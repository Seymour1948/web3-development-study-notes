Smart Contract Life Cycle

Deploy

sendTransaction({from:..., to:empty, data:'0x123'});

Smart Contract becomes his own address

Interact

sendTransaction({to:smartContractAddress, data:'0x123', value:1000000});

data field containts encoded interaction for the Smart Contract.

Destroy/Stop

Call "selfdestruct(beneficiaryAddress)" inside a Solidity function

Smart Contract will not be available in any blocks afterwards anymore
